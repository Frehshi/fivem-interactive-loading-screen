Cosmic State Roleplay

Welcome to Cosmic State Roleplay! This is a place where you can do whatever you want and build your own story.

Join one of our departments and work your way up, whether that’s law enforcement, DOT, or another public service role. Want to live on the edge instead? Become a criminal, run from the law, plan jobs, and make a name for yourself. Or just chill as a civilian, get a job, start a business, meet people, and live your life in the city.

We focus on realistic, fun roleplay with an active community and staff that actually care. Whether you’re brand new to roleplay or have been doing it for years, you’ll fit right in here.

Jump in, get creative, and see where your story takes you.
